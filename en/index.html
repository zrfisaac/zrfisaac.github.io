My Journey as a Software Developer

Hello! I’m Isaac Caires, a passionate software developer with over 15 years of experience. My journey began in 2005, when I first dove into the world of programming, working on GSI, an ERP system originally developed by my father. This was where I honed my skills in Delphi, SQL Server, and report generation, forming the foundation of my career.

As I progressed, I joined Techsallus, where I expanded my knowledge of Delphi and SQL Server, particularly in the development and maintenance of hospital management systems. My work there involved optimizing stored procedures, creating jobs and functions, and troubleshooting complex database issues, which gave me valuable experience in large-scale system management.

In 2022, I moved to Softwell Solutions, where I made a significant impact on the Maker Platform. One of my proudest achievements was adapting the platform to run on GNU/Linux using Wine, making it more memory-efficient and cost-effective for clients. This was a game-changer that also allowed the platform to be accessed remotely via the web. I continued my work with a variety of tools and technologies such as Docker, PostgreSQL, Oracle, and Delphi, while always focusing on clean, maintainable code and cross-platform compatibility.

Throughout my career, I’ve been driven by a desire to learn, grow, and apply the latest technologies to solve real-world problems. I take pride in following best practices, such as SOLID principles and design patterns, and in constantly pushing the boundaries of what I can do. Today, I continue to look for new opportunities to leverage my skills and help others bring their ideas to life.
