## About Me
Hello! I'm a software developer passionate about creating innovative and efficient solutions. With over 12 years of experience in the field, I focus my expertise on Delphi and ASP.NET MVC languages. Currently, I'm at Softwell, where I contribute as one of the developers of the Maker tool, used for visually and rapidly developing ERP systems, with a special focus on database integration. My proficiency spans across a variety of database platforms, including Oracle, PostgreSQL, SQL Server, MySQL, and Firebird, ensuring the delivery of robust and scalable solutions.
